About Us

Located in the center of District 3, Ho Chi Minh City, Pho Mien Ga Ky Dong is proud to be a part of the flourishing food scene in the most populous city in Vietnam. We are honored to have won the Michelin Guide's Bib Gourmand Award in 2023 and 2024, which recognizes our dedication to quality. This recognition honors our commitment to providing outstanding tastes and exceptional eating experiences.

At Pho Mien Ga Ky Dong, we are proud to provide some of the tastiest chicken noodle soup in Ho Chi Minh City. Our meals are carefully prepared using only the freshest, highest-quality meats and locally gathered ingredients from trusted markets. Beyond our traditional chicken pho, we have several kinds of noodle and salad alternatives to suit every customers. To complete your meal, try our refreshing handcrafted drinks or one of our delicious smoothies, which are ideal for any occasion.

Our spacious dining area is excellent for family gatherings, coming together up with friends, or chilling after a long day at work. We are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness and sanitation to guarantee that every meal is not only tasty, but also safe and nutritious. At Pho Mien Ga Ky Dong, we seek to create a welcoming atmosphere in which excellent cuisine and good company combine to create unforgettable memories.
